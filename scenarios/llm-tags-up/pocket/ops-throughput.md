# Ops Tagging Throughput

**As of:** 2026-05-23

## Capacity

- 4 ops associates, dedicated to tagging
- ~80 SKUs/day each = **320 SKUs/day team**
- 250 working days/year = **80,000 SKU-events/year** at full utilization

## Demand

- Active catalog: ~50,000 SKUs
- Monthly churn (new SKUs + revisions): ~12% = ~6,000 SKU-events/month = ~72,000/year
- Capacity is roughly matched to steady-state demand, but with no buffer.

## Backlog

- Backlog 2025-10-01: 600 SKUs (normal seasonal carry)
- Backlog 2026-05-23: **4,800 SKUs**
- Growth rate since Q4 2025: ~600 SKUs/month net

Drivers of backlog growth: Q4 onboarding of 3 new vendors added ~1,200 SKUs above plan; one rater (Priya) on parental leave Feb-Apr.

## Cost

- Loaded labor: $32/hr × 8 hr × 4 people × 250 days = **$256,000/year**
- Excludes benefits multiplier already (loaded rate includes it)
- Excludes manager time on QA / escalations (~5 hr/week for Mei as lead, not separately costed)

## Risk

At current backlog growth rate, by Q4 2026 backlog hits ~8,000 SKUs and starts visibly degrading time-to-list for new vendor product.
