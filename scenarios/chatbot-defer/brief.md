# Proposal: AI Chatbot on the Support Page

We want to add an AI chatbot on the support page to improve customer experience and reduce ticket volume for the support team. A few of us saw a vendor demo from Helprite at SupportConf in Q1 and the deflection numbers looked compelling, especially for a team our size. Our median first response is still around 4 hours and NPS has been stuck around 35 for two quarters, so I think this is the right moment to modernize the support surface and meet customers where they already are. The board has been pushing on AI initiatives since the Q1 review, so this would also satisfy the action item assigned to my function. Helprite quoted us roughly $40K platform plus seat fees, implementation in 6 to 8 weeks, and they cited a 42% deflection figure at comparable SaaS customers. I'd like to move toward signing in the next few weeks so we can pilot before end of Q2.
