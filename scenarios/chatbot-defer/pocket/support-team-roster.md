# Support Team Roster - 2026-Q2

**Headcount:** 25 agents + 2 leadership
**Last updated:** 2026-05-08
**Owner:** Jamie Okafor

## Leadership

- **Jamie Okafor** - Director of Support. Reports to VPCX. Owns staffing, SLAs, escalation policy.
- **Lin Saito** - Head of Customer Operations. Reports to VPCX. Owns tooling (Zendesk, Guide, internal ops), workforce-management forecasting, reporting.

## Senior agents (8)

Handle escalations from T1 queue, mentor frontline, and write/update KB articles in slack time. Roughly 15-20% of their week goes to KB authoring when queue allows, though in practice queue rarely allows.

- Loaded comp range: $46K - $48K
- Tenure: 2 - 5 years
- No one in this group has formal training or charter around prompt engineering, automation tooling, or vendor evaluation.

## Frontline agents (17)

Handle T1 inbound queue (chat + email). Two on weekend rotation.

- Loaded comp range: $42K - $44K
- Tenure: 6 months - 2 years (high turnover band; we backfilled 6 seats in 2025)
- T1 productivity target: 35 tickets/agent/day. Actual: 28 - 32 depending on month.

## Function ownership

- **No one currently owns "support automation" as a charter.** Closest is Lin's ops function, but Lin's mandate is tooling and reporting, not automation product ownership.
- KB ownership is informally distributed across the 8 seniors with no single editor.
- If we sign a chatbot vendor, we have not yet decided who runs the pilot, defines success metrics, or owns the KB cleanup that the pilot would depend on.
