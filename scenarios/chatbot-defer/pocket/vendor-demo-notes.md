# Helprite Vendor Demo - SupportConf 2026-Q1

**Date attended:** 2026-02-18
**Attendees from our side:** VPCX, Jamie Okafor (Director of Support), one senior agent
**Vendor contact:** Priya Ranganathan, AE at Helprite (priya@helprite.io)

## What they showed

- Live sandbox flow on synthetic demo tickets (password reset, billing question, plan-change request)
- Bot resolved 3 of 4 demo tickets without handoff in the booth demo
- Admin console for KB ingestion - they say they can ingest from Zendesk Guide, Intercom Articles, or raw markdown
- Slackbot integration for agent assist (separate SKU, not in base price)

## Claims made on stage and in 1:1

- "42% deflection at comparable SaaS customers"
- "Most customers see payback in under 6 months"
- "Average time-to-first-value: 3 weeks post-implementation"

## What they did NOT show or share

- No named customer reference accounts. Priya said she could "set up a call with a reference" if we move to procurement stage.
- No breakdown of the 42% figure - which ticket categories deflect at what rate, how "deflection" is defined (resolved vs deferred vs abandoned), what the denominator is.
- No benchmarked time-to-deflection or time-to-handoff numbers.
- No churn-impact or CSAT-impact data on deflected tickets.

## Pricing as quoted

- Platform fee: $40,000 / year
- Agent seats: $5 / seat / month, billed annually. At 25 agents that's $1,500/month or $18,000/year.
- Total Y1: ~$58,000 before implementation services.
- Implementation services: $15K - $25K depending on KB volume. They said our ~340 articles is "on the low end of normal."
- Timeline: 6 - 8 weeks from contract signature to soft launch.

## Open questions to ask before signing

- Define "deflection" in their telemetry
- Reference customer of similar ticket-mix and team size
- What happens to CSAT on the deflected cohort vs the human-handled cohort
