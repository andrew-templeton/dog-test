# Board Memo - Q1 2026 Strategic Priorities (Excerpt)

**Date:** 2026-02-04
**Meeting:** Q1 Board Review
**Distribution:** Exec staff, board observers

## Strategic priorities affirmed for FY2026

1. Net revenue retention back above 105% (Product + CS)
2. Reduce CAC payback to under 14 months (GTM)
3. **AI initiatives: identify and pilot customer-facing AI capabilities** (cross-functional)
4. Hiring freeze outside of revenue-generating roles continues through Q2

## Action items from item 3

- CTO: present internal AI/LLM roadmap at Q2 board meeting
- VP Product: identify one product-surface AI feature for pilot
- **VPCX: identify and pilot one customer-facing AI capability by end of Q2 2026**
- CFO: track aggregate AI spend separately in management reporting

## Board commentary captured

Two directors noted that competitors are "putting AI everywhere" and that we risk looking behind. One director cautioned against "AI theater" and asked that pilots have measurable success criteria defined before launch. The board did not specify what those criteria should be.
