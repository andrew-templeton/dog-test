# Pitch: LLM-Generated Search Tags for Product Catalog

We want to deploy an LLM to auto-generate search-term tags across our product catalog. Today the tags are hand-written by our merch ops associates - four people, working through about 80 SKUs per associate per day, and we are still falling behind on the turnover. The hypothesis is that LLM-generated tags will be more comprehensive (synonyms, attribute coverage, long-tail descriptors) than what humans produce under throughput pressure, and that better tags on the result page should lift search-driven conversion. We ran a 1,000-SKU pilot two weeks ago and the numbers came back clean. I am bringing this to you to sanity-check the plan before we scope an 8-10 week rollout across all 50K SKUs. The labor case alone looks defensible; I am mostly here to pressure-test the revenue case.
